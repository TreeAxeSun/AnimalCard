Tiger

The tiger (Panthera tigris) is the largest cat species, most recognizable for their pattern of dark vertical stripes on reddish-orange fur with a lighter underside.

  • Species:: P. tigris
  • Genus: Pantera
  • Family: Felidae
  • Subborder: Feliformia
  • Order:: Carnivora

The species is classified in the genus Panthera with the lion, leopard, jaguar, and snow leopard. Tigers are apex predators, primarily preying on ungulates such as deer and bovids.
